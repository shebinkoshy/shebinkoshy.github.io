Q: Will the app delete my contacts without asking?

No. The app will show you the duplicates it finds and you have full control over which contacts to merge or delete. No contacts are modified without your explicit confirmation.

Q: Does the app upload my contacts to any server?

No. All contact processing happens locally on your device. Your contact data never leaves your device.

Q: The app is not detecting duplicates. What should I do?

Make sure you have granted the app permission to access your contacts in your device settings. Also ensure you have the latest version of the app installed.

Q: How do I report a bug?

Please email us at shebinkoshyios@gmail.com with a description of the bug, your device model, and OS version. Screenshots are helpful!

Q: Is my data safe?

Yes! Your contacts are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded to any server. Please read our Privacy Policy for more details.
