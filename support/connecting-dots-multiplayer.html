Q: I can't connect with other players. What should I do?

Make sure both players have a stable internet connection and the latest version of the app. Try restarting the app and your internet connection. If the problem persists, please contact us.

Q: The game is crashing or not loading. What should I do?

Try the following steps: 1) Make sure you have the latest version installed. 2) Close and reopen the app. 3) Restart your device. 4) Check that you have enough storage space. If the problem persists, please contact us.

Q: How do I report a bug?

Please email us at shebinkoshyios@gmail.com with a description of the bug, your device model, and OS version. Screenshots are helpful!
