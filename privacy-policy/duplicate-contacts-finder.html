Last updated: February 23, 2026

Shebin Koshy ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Duplicate Contacts Finder mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contacts Data: The App accesses your device's contact list to identify and manage duplicate contacts. Contact data is processed locally on your device and is NOT uploaded to our servers.

  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for analytics and crash reporting.
  • Advertising Data: Our App may use third-party advertising services (Google AdMob) that collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads.

2. How We Use Your Information

3. Data Storage

Your contact data is processed entirely on your device. We do NOT store, transmit, or have access to your contact information on any external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Our App may use the following third-party services that collect information:
