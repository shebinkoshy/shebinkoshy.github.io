Privacy Policy for Connecting Dots

Last updated: March 5, 2026

As set forth in our Terms of Use, 'Connecting Dots' is a general audience app, is not targeted to children, and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16 years of age. We encourage minors to seek the consent from their parents before providing any information about themselves.

This Privacy Policy describes generally 'Connecting Dots' application's practices for handling non-personally identifiable information (e.g., user interests, demographics and app usage) to the extent such information is associated with a particular user or user account. We are using third party analytics tools for the improvement of the application and those informations are not tied up with a user. This Privacy Policy applies to all "users".

Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services for analytics and crash reporting:

  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymous usage data such as app sessions, screen views, and user interactions to help us understand how the app is used and improve the experience. This data is not personally identifiable. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Automatically collects crash reports including device model, OS version, and stack traces when the app encounters an error. This helps us identify and fix issues to improve app stability. No personally identifiable information is collected. Firebase Privacy Policy
